Each heartbeat changes your skin color by a fraction of a percent — remote photoplethysmography (POS, Wang et al. 2017). PulseCanvas reads it across your face and renders it live.

Hold still in even light; the reading locks in after ~10–15 s. Cross-check it against your wrist.
